    * Integrated `TargetIcon` into `GameBoard` to represent planned bombardments on target pieces.
    * Removed manual crosshair drawing from `BombardPainter`, which now only draws the dashed connection lines.
    * Removed the "Cancel" `[x]` button from `PlanningPanel` and kept the `gps_fixed` crosshair icon active during targeting mode.
    * Ensured that targeting mode is cancelled when tapping an illegal square or deselecting the piece.

    * Modified `_handlePieceTap` in `GameBoard` to remove all pending actions (moves, bombards, placements) for a piece when it is deselected.
    * Updated piece selection logic to allow selecting pieces that were just placed, specifically to enable deselecting them to cancel their placement.

    * Implemented `ReplayPanel` with toggleable buttons (1-7) to navigate through the turn resolution steps.
    * Created a suite of event-specific overlay widgets in `lib/features/game/widgets/event_widgets/`:

        * `BattleIcon`: A custom SVG lightning bolt icon for representing combat.
        * `TargetIcon`: Used for representing bombardment targets.
    * Enhanced `GameplayUiState` to manage `selectedEvent` and `currentReplayStep`.
    * Implemented `ReplayArrowPainter` to visualize unit movement during Step 1, including support for opposing ships and fog of war visibility.
    * Integrated event selection logic into `GameReplayBoard`, allowing users to tap affected squares to view detailed event outcome cards.
    * Updated `GamePlay` screen to fetch turn events and integrate the new replay interface.

        * Step 2: State after bombardment resolution.

        * Step 4: State after battle destruction.
        * Step 5: State after resolution movement and city captures.

    * Updated `TurnEventList` model and `GameBoard` to handle the new snapshot data.
